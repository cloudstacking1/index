What Is Water Damage?

Water damage is any type of destruction caused by water intrusion. This may include:

Flooding from natural disasters

Overflowing toilets, sinks, or bathtubs

Leaking pipes or appliances

Roof leaks after storms

Sewer backup

No matter the cause, it’s essential to act fast. Water seeps into walls, floors, and belongings, weakening structural integrity and creating the perfect environment for mold growth.
